Indo-Chinese Vegetable Fried Rice

Restaurant-style vegetable fried rice with crispy veggies and aromatic flavors - ready in just 20 minutes!

    Instructions

    1. Cook and cool the rice

      Cook basmati rice with 1¾ cups water until done. Spread on a plate and cool completely for at least 1 hour. Cold, day-old rice works best for fried rice as it's less sticky.

    2. Prep the vegetables

      Finely chop all vegetables into small, uniform pieces. Keep carrots, beans, cabbage, bell peppers, and spring onions separate for sequential cooking.

    3. Heat wok on high

      Heat oil in a wok or large pan on high heat until smoking hot. High heat is essential for authentic wok flavor and to prevent vegetables from becoming soggy.

    4. Stir-fry aromatics

      Add ginger and garlic, stir-fry for 10 seconds. Add green chilies and white part of spring onions, stir-fry for 30 seconds until fragrant.

    5. Cook vegetables in order

      Add carrots and beans first, stir-fry 2 minutes. Add cabbage and bell peppers, stir-fry 2 more minutes. Vegetables should be crisp, not soft. Keep stirring constantly on high heat.

    6. Add sauces and seasonings

      Push vegetables to the side. Add soy sauce, vinegar, and pepper in the center. Let it sizzle for 5 seconds, then mix with vegetables. Add salt to taste.

    7. Add rice and toss

      Add the cooled rice, breaking any clumps with a spatula. Toss everything together on high heat for 3-4 minutes until rice is heated through and slightly crispy. Keep tossing to prevent sticking.

    8. Finish and serve

      Add green part of spring onions and toss for 30 seconds. Turn off heat. Garnish with more spring onions if desired. Serve immediately while hot with chili sauce or manchurian gravy.
