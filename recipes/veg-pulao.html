One-Pot Pressure Cooker Veg Pulao

Easy vegetable pulao made in a pressure cooker - just layer everything and let it cook to perfection!

4

Ingredients

    Instructions

    1. Rinse the rice

      Wash basmati rice in cold water 2-3 times until water runs clear. Soak for 15 minutes, then drain completely.

    2. Layer ingredients

      Add ghee or oil. Add cumin seeds, bay leaf, cinnamon, cloves, cardamom. Add onion at bottom.

    3. Add vegetables

      Add ginger-garlic paste, chilies, tomato, mixed vegetables evenly.

    4. Add rice & masala

      Add drained rice. Sprinkle salt, turmeric, chili powder, garam masala.

    5. Pressure cook

      Add water. Do not stir. Cook at low pressure 3 minutes after steam release.

    6. Natural release

      Allow natural pressure release 10–15 minutes.

    7. Fluff & serve

      Fluff gently and serve hot.
