Hyderabadi Veg Dum Biryani

Authentic layered dum-style Hyderabadi vegetable biryani with fried onions, mint, saffron and slow steam cooking.

Vegetarian Rice Dish Hyderabadi Dum Cooking
4

Ingredients

    Instructions

    1. Marinate vegetables

      Mix vegetables with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, chili powder, turmeric, biryani masala, salt, mint, coriander and half the fried onions. Rest 30 minutes.

    2. Cook rice 70%

      Boil water with whole spices and salt. Add soaked rice and cook until 70% done. Drain immediately.

    3. Layering

      In heavy pot, spread marinated vegetables at bottom. Layer partially cooked rice over it.

    4. Top layers

      Add saffron milk, remaining fried onions, mint, coriander and drizzle ghee.

    5. Dum cooking

      Seal lid tightly. Cook on low flame 20–25 minutes. Rest 10 minutes before opening.

    6. Serve

      Gently fluff from side. Serve with raita or mirchi ka salan.
