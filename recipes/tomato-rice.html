South Indian Tomato Rice

Flavorful, mildly spicy tomato rice made with aromatic tempering and perfectly cooked rice.

Vegetarian Rice Dish South Indian Quick Meal
4

Ingredients

    Instructions

    1. Cook and cool the rice

      Cook rice until fluffy and separate. Spread on a plate and cool completely to prevent clumping.

    2. Prepare tempering

      Heat oil in a pan. Add mustard seeds and let them splutter. Add cumin seeds and curry leaves.

    3. Cook onion and tomatoes

      Add sliced onions and sauté until soft. Add ginger-garlic paste and green chilies. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until soft and oil separates.

    4. Add spices

      Add turmeric, chili powder, garam masala, and salt. Mix well and cook 2 minutes.

    5. Mix rice

      Add cooled rice and gently mix until evenly coated with masala. Cook on low heat 3–4 minutes.

    6. Finish and serve

      Garnish with fresh coriander leaves. Serve hot with raita or papad.
