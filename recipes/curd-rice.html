South Indian Curd Rice

Cooling, creamy and lightly tempered curd rice — the ultimate South Indian comfort dish.

Vegetarian Rice Dish South Indian Comfort Food
4

Ingredients

    Instructions

    1. Prepare rice base

      Lightly mash cooked rice while still warm. Add milk and mix. Let cool completely.

    2. Add yogurt

      Add thick yogurt and salt. Mix until creamy and smooth.

    3. Prepare tempering

      Heat oil. Add mustard seeds. When they splutter, add cumin, urad dal, chana dal, red chilies, green chili, ginger and curry leaves.

    4. Combine

      Pour tempering over curd rice and mix well.

    5. Finish

      Garnish with coriander and optional pomegranate seeds. Chill slightly before serving.
